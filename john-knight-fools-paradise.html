John Knight is a conceptual artist based in Missoula, MT. Knight holds a MFA in Visual Studies from Pacific Northwest College of Art, Portland, OR. His work is concerned with class identity and its intersection with design and architecture. Knight has presented solo projects at the Holland Project, Reno, NV; Final Hot Desert, Salt Lake City, UT; Private Places and Muscle Beach, Portland; the American Institute of Thoughts and Feelings, Tucson, AZ; and Julius Caesar, Chicago, IL. Upcoming solo efforts include an exhibition at BSMNT Gallery in Leipzig, DK. Recent Curatorial projects include: Williamson | Knight and Cherry and Lucic, Portland; and THE PINK HOUSE (Jan. 19 1995) at Bad Reputation, Los Angeles, CA. Currently, he works at the Missoula Art Museum as a Registrar, and serves on the board of directors for KALICO Art Center in Kalispell, MT.
