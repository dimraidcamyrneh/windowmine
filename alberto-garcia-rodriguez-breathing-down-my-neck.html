Alberto Garcia Rodriguez

Breathing Down My Neck
April 12 – 30, 2021

instagram.com/bertobro

Breathing Down My Neck is part of creating polymorphic narratives from mixing cliche personal ephemera and found imagery. Through this process, the work is colliding into a possibility of new analysis and transgression.

Alberto Garcia Rodriguez (b.1994, Reno, Nevada) is a multidisciplinary artist and curator working in Reno, Nevada. His work often plays with themes of memory, humor, and hysteria.
