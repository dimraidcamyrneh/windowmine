Noah Greene

The House is Shallow
October 5 – 30, 2020

noahgreene.com

The house is shallow
we’ve seen the low sun from both directions never setting

an unceasing present thins
and thins

The house leached love from the river

Noah Greene (b. 1984) lives and works in rural Oregon. He received his MFA from the University of California Davis in 2018. Noah’s practice includes painting, sculpture and other peripheral media; arrangements in muted conversation with the spaces they inhabit.
